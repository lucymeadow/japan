16 days · Nov 23 – Dec 9, 2018

The actual day-by-day.

A reference for pacing — not a script. Skip days, swap nights, do what you want.

Tokyo, part one

Day 1Sat · Nov 24

Arrive Narita · settle in Shinjuku

  • Land at Narita Terminal 2 mid-afternoon. The trip from Narita into central Tokyo is ~75 min on the Narita Express or ~90 min on the limousine bus.
  • Check in at Hotel Gracery Shinjuku in Kabukicho. Eat ramen, sleep early.
Day 2Sun · Nov 25

Shimo-Kitazawa, Shibuya, Harajuku, Meiji

  • Morning in Shimo-Kitazawa — thrift, coffee, a stop at mois cafe.
  • Mid-afternoon: hit Shibuya Crossing, then walk up to Harajuku for Takeshita Street and Totti Candy Factory.
  • End the loop at Meiji Shrine at the golden hour.
Day 3Mon · Nov 26

Tokyo Station, Ginza, Robot Restaurant night

  • Morning at Tokyo Station and the Imperial Palace grounds.
  • Afternoon in Ginza.
  • Dinner at Shinjuku Warayakiya for straw-fired bonito, then Robot Restaurant at 9pm. (The original closed; an Samurai Restaurant successor now operates the same shows in roughly the same venue.)
Day 4Tue · Nov 27

Tsukiji breakfast, Roppongi rooftop, Yasuda dinner

  • Early breakfast wandering Tsukiji Outer Market — sashimi at Nakaya.
  • Sunset from Tokyo City View & Sky Deck at Roppongi Hills.
  • Dinner at Sushi Bar Yasuda in Minato. Counter omakase.

Hakone

Day 5Wed · Nov 28

Tokyo → Hakone

Tokyo Station → Odawara on Kodama 655 (~35 min) → Hakone Tozan line → Laforet Club Hakone Gora. Onsen → yukata → kaiseki dinner → sleep.

Kyoto

Day 6Thu · Nov 29

Hakone → Kyoto · Pontocho dinner

Odawara → Kyoto on Hikari 515 (~2 hr) → The Royal Park Hotel Kyoto Sanjo.
  • Evening walk down Pontocho, dinner along the alley.
Day 7Fri · Nov 30

Private Kyoto day · Fushimi Inari at dusk

  • 9am: 6-hour private tour with a local guide (ToursByLocals) — Ryoan-ji, Kinkaku-ji, Kiyomizu-dera.
  • Late afternoon: Fushimi Inari — the torii gates are best at golden hour with thinner crowds.
  • Dinner at Yamafuku for shabu-shabu.
Day 8Sat · Dec 1

Arashiyama morning · Nishiki lunch · maiko evening

  • Early start to Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, then up to the Monkey Park Iwatayama for the city view.
  • Afternoon back downtown: Nishiki Market and a wander through Gion.
  • Evening: Maiko performance at Yasakadori Enraku (booked via Veltra).
Day 9Sun · Dec 2

Day trip: Nara

Kyoto → Nara on the JR Nara line (~45 min). Deer in Nara Park, the great Buddha at Todai-ji, lunch, train back.
  • Dinner back in Kyoto at 二寧坂 鮨沼本 (Nineizaka Sushi Numamoto) in Higashiyama.

Osaka & Koyasan

Day 10Mon · Dec 3

Kyoto → Osaka · Dotonbori

Kyoto → Osaka on JR (~15 min) → Fraser Residence Nankai Osaka in Namba.
  • Evening grazing through Dotonbori; cap it with Ichiran Dotonbori Honkan for ramen.
Day 11Tue · Dec 4

Kuromon lunch · Osaka → Koyasan

  • Morning at Kuromon Ichiba Market. Eat your way through.
Namba → Koyasan on the Nankai Koya line (~90 min) → cable car → bus into town → check in at Koyasan Syukubo Ekoin. Shojin-ryori dinner served in the room.
  • 9pm: monk-led night cemetery tour through Okunoin. (Required highlight.)
Day 12Wed · Dec 5

Full day on Mt. Koya

  • Morning fire ceremony (goma) at Ekoin.
  • Okunoin again at sunrise — misty and empty, completely different from the night before.
  • Walk the two main temple complexes (Garan, Kongobu-ji).
  • Second night back at Ekoin.

Tokyo, part two

Day 13Thu · Dec 6

Koyasan → Tokyo

Koyasan → Shin-Osaka (bus → cable car → Nankai line, ~2 hr) → Shin-Osaka → Tokyo Station on Hikari 520 (~3 hr) → Keio Plaza Hotel Tokyo in West Shinjuku.
Day 14Fri · Dec 7

Tokyo DisneySea

  • Whole day at Tokyo DisneySea. Park opens 9am, plan to stay through fireworks.
Day 15Sat · Dec 8

Odaiba · teamLab

  • Morning in Odaiba: Rainbow Bridge views, the Gundam, mall wandering.
  • Afternoon at teamLab (2 hours minimum). Note: the original Borderless venue closed in 2022; it reopened at Azabudai Hills in early 2024.
Day 16Sun · Dec 9

Fly home

  • Late-morning checkout. Limousine bus or N'EX out to Narita for evening departure.

If you only have 10 days

Cut Hakone and the second Koyasan night. Compress Tokyo part-two to two days. You can still keep all six cities and the temple stay.
